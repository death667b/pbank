What is Piggy Bank

This new tool will allow you to create sub-accounts with your bank. Which will help to ensure you're not caught unaware by unexpected bills plus more.

  • Micro accounts for rent, food, cloths etc
  • Automatic payment to these accounts when paid
  • Pay directly out of these accounts

Wouldn't that be great?

This way, you don't spend all of your rent money on beer or cloths!
